About Us

Bringing Musical Excellence to Sunyani

Founded in 2021, Mellow Octave Brass Band has become a cornerstone of the cultural landscape in Sunyani and the surrounding Odumase community. Our ensemble of talented musicians specializes in traditional Ghanaian brass music with contemporary influences.

We pride ourselves on our rich sound, precise harmonies, and dynamic performances that captivate audiences at weddings, festivals, national celebrations, and community events throughout the Brong-Ahafo region.

Our mission is to preserve and promote Ghanaian musical heritage while creating unforgettable experiences through the power of brass music.
