Join Mellow Octave Brass Band

Bring your talent to Sunyani's premier brass ensemble. We're looking for passionate musicians to join our family.

Membership Requirements

  • Proficiency in playing at least one brass instrument (trumpet, trombone, tuba, etc.)
  • Ability to read musical notation
  • Commitment to regular rehearsals (typically twice weekly)
  • Willingness to perform at scheduled events throughout the year
  • Passion for Ghanaian musical traditions and brass band culture
  • Residency in or near Sunyani/Odumase area
